Festival 1: Ram Navami

May

Saga Dawa (May 23): A holy Buddhist festival marking the birth, enlightenment, and death of Lord Buddha.

June

Festival 1: Saga Dawa

Festival 2: Monsoon Festival

July

Festival 1: Hemis Festival

Festival 2: Rainy Festival

August

Pang Lhabsol (August 15): Celebrates Mount Kanchenjunga as the guardian deity of Sikkim.

September

Festival 1: Onam

Festival 2: Autumn Festival

October

Dasain (Dussehra) (October 20): A major Hindu festival celebrated with family gatherings and rituals.

November

Tihar (Diwali) (November 12): Festival of lights celebrated with prayers, decorations, and feasts.

December

Losoong (December 18): Marks the Sikkimese New Year and the end of the harvest season.

Namsoong (December 24): Celebrated by the Lepcha community as their New Year.
